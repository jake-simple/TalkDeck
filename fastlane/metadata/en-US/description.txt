IceDeck is a card game app that sparks meaningful conversations with thoughtfully crafted question cards.

The perfect icebreaker deck for any relationship — friends, couples, family, or coworkers!

16 Card Packs
- Situation-specific packs including Friends, Couple, Family, Icebreaking, Balance Game, and more
- 240 carefully curated question cards per pack
- 4 categories per pack covering diverse topics

13 Visual Themes
- Unique card designs inspired by trading card games like Yu-Gi-Oh, Pokemon, MTG, and Tarot
- Animated theme backgrounds (snow, stars, bubbles, and more)
- Set the mood with your favorite theme

Intuitive Card Play
- Swipe to flip through cards
- Shake to shuffle the deck
- Random card draw for unpredictable fun

When the conversation stalls, when you want to know someone better, when the mood needs a boost — pull out IceDeck.
